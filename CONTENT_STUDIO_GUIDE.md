# Content Studio User Guide

## Overview
The Content Studio is your AI-powered workspace for creating professional English learning content for tech professionals. It integrates three powerful AI APIs (Gemini, OpenAI, and Perplexity) to generate high-quality educational templates.

## How to Access
1. Navigate to **Content Studio** in the main navigation
2. The platform automatically authenticates you as a demo user
3. Start creating content immediately

## Key Features

### 1. AI Content Ideas Generator
- **Purpose**: Get inspiring content ideas for your English learning materials
- **How to use**: 
  - Click "Generate Content Ideas" button
  - AI analyzes current tech trends and generates relevant topics
  - Examples: "Docker Communication for DevOps Teams", "AI/ML Technical Writing"

### 2. Multi-Modal Content Creation
Choose from different content types:
- **Interactive Course**: Full learning modules with exercises
- **Video Script**: Structured scripts for educational videos  
- **Workshop Materials**: Hands-on training content
- **Assessment**: Tests and quizzes for learners

### 3. Smart Form System
The content generator uses intelligent forms to capture:
- **Content Type**: What kind of material you're creating
- **Target Audience**: Skill level (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced)
- **Topic**: Specific subject matter
- **Learning Objectives**: What students should achieve
- **Duration**: How long the content should take
- **Additional Requirements**: Special needs or preferences

### 4. AI-Powered Generation Process
- **Multi-API Integration**: Uses Gemini, OpenAI, and Perplexity for best results
- **Real-time Progress**: Watch as your content is being created
- **Quality Optimization**: AI ensures content meets educational standards

### 5. Content Preview & Editing
- **Live Preview**: See your generated content immediately
- **Edit Mode**: Make adjustments and refinements
- **Export Options**: Save as template or download

### 6. Template Creation
- **One-Click Publishing**: Turn your content into marketplace templates
- **Automatic Pricing**: AI suggests optimal pricing based on content complexity
- **Instant Deployment**: Templates become available in the marketplace

## Step-by-Step Workflow

1. **Start with Ideas** (Optional)
   - Click "Generate Content Ideas" for inspiration
   - Browse suggested topics relevant to tech English learning

2. **Choose Content Type**
   - Select from Interactive Course, Video Script, Workshop, or Assessment
   - Each type generates different content structures

3. **Fill the Smart Form**
   - Target Audience: Choose skill level
   - Topic: Enter your specific subject
   - Learning Objectives: Define what students will learn
   - Duration: Set expected completion time
   - Requirements: Add any special instructions

4. **Generate Content**
   - Click "Generate Content" 
   - Watch real-time progress as AI creates your material
   - Process typically takes 10-30 seconds

5. **Review & Edit**
   - Preview the generated content
   - Make any necessary adjustments
   - Content includes interactive elements, exercises, and assessments

6. **Publish as Template**
   - Click "Create Template" to add to marketplace
   - AI automatically sets appropriate pricing
   - Your template becomes available for purchase
